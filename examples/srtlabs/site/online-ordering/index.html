What it does

  • Shows customers a menu of items offered by your business
  • Allows your customer to build an order, specify a pickup time, and pay online
  • Tracks each order from payment through fulfillment
  • Intelligently notifies your employees when to execute an order
  • Allows recurring orders on a regular schedule for repeat customers

How it Works

  • Customer orders are placed through a mobile-responsive web-page. This can be hosted on the your existing web site. Or on an SRT-hosted page (orders.yourbusiness.com)
  • Completed orders are sent to SRT’s server for processing and credit card charging
  • Tablet computer at your location displays completed orders a specified amount of time before they are due for collection
  • Employee swipes on tablet when order is complete (if customer wishes to be notified) and when it is picked up

How the Money Works

  • All credit card transactions are handled by Stripe (www.stripe.com)
  • Stripe charges 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
  • SRT charges an additional 1.1% + $0.10 per transaction
  • Money is deposited in client bank account on a rolling 7-day basis.

Compare:

  • Breadcrumb: 1.8% + $0.15/swipe (+hardware/acct fees)
  • Square: 2.75% + $0.00/swipe + (+hardware/acct fees)
  • Braintree: 2.9% + $0.30/trans (no add. fees)
  • PayPal: 2.9% + $0.30/trans (no add. fees)

How This Helps Your Customers

  • They don’t have to wait in line to order
  • They can be en route to your business as you make their order
  • They can save their regular order and not have to enter it every time
  • They can pay by credit card without having to find their card, swipe, and sign.

How This Helps You

  • A happy customer is a repeat customer
  • You can easily track customer habits
  • Your employees don’t have to spend as much time working the register
  • You can target marketing at repeat customers
  • Customers bypassing cashiers means you can service more customers
  • Online ordering provides a potential new revenue stream
